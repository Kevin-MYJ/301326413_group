Sudoku Game

Sudoku is a mathematical game originated in Switzerland in the 18th century. It is a logic game that uses paper and pen to calculate. Players need to infer the numbers of all remaining spaces based on the known numbers on the 9Ã—9 board.

Standard Sudoku rules

Fill in any number from 1 to 9 in the blank, so that the numbers in each row, column, and square thick-lined nine-square grid area on the disk all contain 1 to 9, without repeated numbers.
